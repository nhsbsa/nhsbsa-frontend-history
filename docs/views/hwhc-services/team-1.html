Low income scheme

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

If you have a low income, the NHS Low Income Scheme could help you pay for:

  • NHS prescription charges
  • NHS dental treatment charges
  • the cost of sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • the cost of travelling to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports (check with your hospital for their arrangements for supplying NHS wigs)

Assessor view

The assessor view is built into LIS and is used by a team of assessors at Bridge house, who get a play back of applications in their own dashboard view

Eligibility checker

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

The Eligibility checker is a service that allows users to see if they get help to pay these NHS charges:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
