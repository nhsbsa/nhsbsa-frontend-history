Eligibility checker

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

The Eligibility checker is a service that allows users to see if they get help to pay these NHS charges:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

Low income scheme

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

Description

If you have a low income, the NHS Low Income Scheme could help you pay for:

  • NHS prescription charges
  • NHS dental treatment charges
  • the cost of sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • the cost of travelling to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports (check with your hospital for their arrangements for supplying NHS wigs)

Notes

As of November 2021 there are 339 pages in the LIS application. The pages are created using YAML, which call HTML/Thymeleaf fragments.

LIS is compartmentalised into different sections and each section has a feature flag. When working locally you can enable or disable feature flags depending on which pages you want to see.

You can access this at /apply-for-help-with-nhs-costs/direct/test.

Assessor view

The assessor view is built into LIS and is used by a team of assessors at Bridge house, who get a play back of applications in their own dashboard view.

The check your answers screens the users sees are played back to the assessors in the assessor view. It's the same HTML, but we use custom CSS to change the display in the assessor view.

It's on the roadmap to decouple the assessor view from LIS.

You can access this at /apply-for-help-with-nhs-costs/sign-in.
